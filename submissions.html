Submission Guidelines

We welcome submissions from all aspiring authors. Please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Poetry: No character limit.
  • Short Fiction: Maximum 5000 characters.
  • Nonfiction Essays: Maximum 2000 characters.

Legal Notice

By submitting your work to The Fox and Otter, you grant us the right to publish your work on our website in perpetuity. Copyright remains with the author, and you are free to republish your work elsewhere after one calendar year.
